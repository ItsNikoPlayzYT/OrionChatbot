Privacy Policy
==============

Orion AI Chatbot respects your privacy.

1. Data Collection
   Orion runs locally on your machine. Your chat history and settings are stored in your local AppData folder.

2. AI Processing
   Conversations are processed locally using Ollama. No chat data is sent to external servers by Orion, unless you explicitly use features that require internet access (like checking for updates).

3. Contact
   For questions, please contact the OmniNode team.
